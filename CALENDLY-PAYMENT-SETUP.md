# Calendly Payment Setup Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how to enable payment collection ($29) for your 15-minute Video Diagnosis appointments in Calendly.

---

## Step 1: Log in to Calendly
Go to https://calendly.com and log in to your account.

---

## Step 2: Go to Event Types
1. Click on **"Event Types"** in the left sidebar
2. Find your **"15-Minute Video Diagnosis"** event (or create one if it doesn't exist)
3. Click on the event to edit it

---

## Step 3: Enable Payments
1. In the event settings, look for **"Payments"** or **"Collect Payments"** section
2. Toggle it **ON**

---

## Step 4: Connect Stripe or PayPal

### Option A: Stripe (Recommended)
1. Click **"Connect with Stripe"**
2. Log in to your Stripe account (or create one at https://stripe.com)
3. Authorize Calendly to accept payments
4. Done! Stripe will handle all card payments

### Option B: PayPal
1. Click **"Connect with PayPal"**
2. Log in to your PayPal business account
3. Authorize Calendly
4. Done!

---

## Step 5: Set the Price
1. Enter **$29.00** (or **29 AUD**) as the price
2. Add a description: "15-Minute Video Diagnosis with Licensed Plumber"
3. Save changes

---

## Step 6: Test the Booking Flow
1. Open your Calendly booking link in a new browser (incognito mode)
2. Select a time slot
3. Fill in the details
4. Verify that the payment step appears
5. Use a test card if using Stripe test mode

---

## How It Works for Customers
1. Customer clicks "Book a Video Diagnosis" on your website
2. They select an available time slot
3. They enter their contact details
4. They see the $29 payment screen
5. They pay via card (Stripe) or PayPal
6. Booking is confirmed and both parties receive email confirmations

---

## Notes
- Calendly takes no cut of your payments
- Stripe fees: ~2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- PayPal fees: ~2.9% + fixed fee per transaction
- You can enable refunds directly in Stripe/PayPal dashboard
